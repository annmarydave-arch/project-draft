Tropical Mix

A bright, naturally sweet mixed-fruit juice combining mango, pineapple, orange, and strawberries. Smooth, refreshing, and perfect for breakfast or parties.

Prep: 7 min Serves: 2–3

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 orange
  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup cold water or ice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Steps

  1. Add all fruits into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add water or ice if needed.
  4. Taste and adjust sweetness.
  5. Serve cold and enjoy.

Tools

  • Blender
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Strawberry Splash

A delicious strawberry drink packed with natural sweetness, vitamins, and refreshing flavor for hot days.

Prep: 5 min Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups strawberries
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Wash the strawberries.
  2. Add ingredients into a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Serve cold.

Pineapple Fresh

Pineapple Fresh is a tropical juice filled with sweet flavor, vitamin C, and natural energy.

Prep: 6 min Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add pineapple into blender.
  2. Blend with water and ice.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness.
  4. Serve chilled.
